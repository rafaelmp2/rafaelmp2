Rafael Pina - Bio

Hi! I am Rafael and I am a PhD student at Loughborough University London. I have started my journey at the University of Coimbra, in Portugal, where I got my BSc in Informatics Engineering. I then came to Loughborough University London to pursue a MSc degree, where I have also worked as a Research Assistant during my masters. After completing my masters degree I started my PhD at Loughborough University London, where I also assist in teaching.
